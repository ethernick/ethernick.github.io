my eyes clot up with fibered remnants
congealed with honeyed yams.
a broken well, the rotten bucket leaks, leaving water to drip. unturned and stale,
pungent fumes billow
industrial green cotton balls,
spitting deep in my eye leaving them swollen and wet.
