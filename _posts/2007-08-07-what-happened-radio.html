When TV is becoming one of the goto places for new music, radio had better keep up or die (some wonder if it’s dead already).

Reasons I’m going into radio:

  1. Show people new, good music - radio has some how turned out to be ass backwards. What happened to Wolfman? “Jack” is halfway there.
  2. CANCON CANCON - There are a whack of great Canadian Talent. 30% isn’t enough anymore!
  3. What about us 20 somethings? 30 somethings? - Musical tastes can solidify, but if soccer moms have Crave FM, then anyone can find “new” bands that meet “old” tastes. See point #1!
